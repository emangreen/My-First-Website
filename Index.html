Different Tennis Shots

Serve

The serve is one of the most important shots in tennis. It is a great shot that can win a lot of matches. The serve always starts each point during a match.

Forehand

The forehand is typically the easier shot to hit and more players hit is very hard. About 75% of grounstrokes are forehands which is much more than the backhand.

the sporting blog, CC BY-NC-SA 2.0


Background of Tennis

Tennis was first invented in the late 1870s and has been growing ever since. Advancements in technology have developed better rackets, strings, and other various tennis accessories which has allowed players to improve each and every year.


Backhand

The backhand is typically the safe shot so there is less risk, but also less reward if the backhand is used a lot throughout a match.

Volley

The volley is probably the most technical shot in tennis and it used by someone who is winning the point. A player typically volleys after a fast serve, forehand, or backhand to finish off the point.
